I am currently a master student at [Southeast University](https://www.seu.edu.cn/), as well as at [Monash University](https://www.monash.edu/), suprvised by [Prof. Jieyue He](https://cse.seu.edu.cn/2019/0105/c23024a257498/page.htm). My research interests encompasses multiple domains, including electronic health record data mining, molecular representation and generation, and the generation of traditional Chinese medicine prescriptions. I aspire to delve deeper into various facets of the biomedical informatics domain, including medical data mining, AI-driven drug discovery, and other related fields.
